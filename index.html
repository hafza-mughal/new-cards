Exhaust Fan

An exhaust fan is utilized to expel air from indoors to outdoors, enhancing ventilation and air
quality. It effectively removes impurities, heat, and odors from enclosed spaces, such as bathrooms,
kitchens, factories, and warehouses. By extracting stale air and introducing fresh air, it helps maintain a clean
and healthy environment, reducing moisture and harmful gases. Installing an exhaust fan promotes air circulation,
contributing to a more comfortable and pleasant atmosphere in homes or businesses.
